Tender and Juicy Grilled Chicken Breast

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 5 large garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 lemons, zested and juiced
  • ⅓ cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for oiling the grill
  • Kosher salt
  • ¼ cup chopped Italian parsley leaves, for garnish

Steps

  1. Use a kitchen mallet to pound the chicken until it’s evenly flattened to about ½-inch.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the Italian seasoning, paprika, red pepper flakes (if using), garlic, lemon zest and juice.
  3. Add the olive oil and whisk to combine.
  4. Season the chicken well all over with salt.
  5. Add the chicken to the bowl with the marinade and toss to coat.
  6. Let it sit at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  7. Lightly oil the grates and preheat your gas grill or an indoor griddle to medium.
  8. Arrange the chicken in one single layer over the heated surface.
  9. Grill for about 2 to 3 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches somewhere between 155°F and 160°F.
  10. Transfer to a large plate and immediately tent a large piece of foil loosely over the chicken.
  11. Allow to rest, covered, until its internal temperature reaches 165°F, about 5 minutes.
  12. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
